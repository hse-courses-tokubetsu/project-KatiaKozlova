joogle is a searcher of russian-language bad jokes.

how it works?

u write down your serching query in russian,

u choose one of the rankers (bm-25, word2vec or navec),

u can change the output number of jokes (default number is 10),

u push the serching button (with a magnifying glass),

u get your top-n results and a time spent on the query.
